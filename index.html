Roller Coaster Track Geometry

The curvature of a roller coaster track is critical for passenger comfort and safety.

Mathematical Modeling

We can model the track as a parametric curve:

x(t) = t, y(t) = sin(t), z(t) = cos(2t)

Here, t represents the position along the track.

Curvature Analysis

Assumptions

  • Track is rigid and smooth
  • Passenger mass is standard
  • Ignore air resistance
  • Curvature below recommended limits ensures comfort

Validation

Compare curvature values to safe g-force limits (e.g., < 3g for amusement rides).
